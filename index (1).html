About Us

Your Trusted Travel Partner in Sri Lanka

At Thinu Cabs & Tours, we believe every journey through Sri Lanka should be as extraordinary as the destination itself. With over a decade of experience serving international travelers, we have perfected the art of creating seamless, luxurious, and culturally rich travel experiences across this beautiful island nation.

Our team of professional, English-speaking drivers are not just chauffeurs — they are passionate local guides who know every hidden gem, secret viewpoint, and authentic eatery along your route. From the golden beaches of the south coast to the misty mountains of the central highlands, we ensure every moment of your Sri Lankan adventure is unforgettable.

What We Offer

Our Premium Services

From airport pickups to multi-day tours, we provide a complete range of premium travel services tailored for international visitors exploring Sri Lanka.

Airport Transfers

Reliable meet-and-greet service at Bandaranaike International Airport. We track your flight and ensure a comfortable, hassle-free transfer to any destination in Sri Lanka.

  • Flight tracking included
  • Meet & greet at arrivals
  • Complimentary water & Wi-Fi

Day Tours

Expertly guided full-day and half-day excursions to Sri Lanka's most iconic attractions including Sigiriya, Kandy, Galle, and the Cultural Triangle.

  • Flexible departure times
  • Knowledgeable local guides
  • Customizable itineraries

Multi-Day Packages

Comprehensive tour packages spanning 3 to 14 days, covering the best of Sri Lanka's cultural heritage, natural wonders, wildlife, and pristine beaches.

  • Hotel pickup & drop-off
  • All-inclusive pricing
  • Professional driver-guide

Private Chauffeur

Hire a dedicated, English-speaking driver for your entire stay. Perfect for travelers who want maximum flexibility and personalized attention throughout their trip.

  • Dedicated personal driver
  • Unlimited mileage options
  • Local insider knowledge

Honeymoon & Romance

Specially curated romantic experiences including scenic hill country drives, sunset beach visits, luxury boutique hotel transfers, and candlelit dinner arrangements.

  • Romantic route planning
  • Luxury vehicle upgrade
  • Special surprise setups

Group & Corporate

Reliable group transportation for corporate events, destination weddings, conference delegations, and large travel parties with fleet coordination expertise.

  • Multi-vehicle coordination
